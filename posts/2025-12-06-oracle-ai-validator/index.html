Introduction

Welcome to a deep dive into the fascinating world of Oracle AI within the Knowledge-Based Currency (KBC) ecosystem! Oracle AI serves as the intelligent engine that validates and retrieves knowledge on demand, playing a crucial role in ensuring the accuracy and reliability of information stored on the K-Chain. In this blog post, we will explore the inner workings of Oracle AI and its significance in shaping the decentralized knowledge economy envisioned by KBC.

Overview

  • Key Principles of Oracle AI: Oracle AI is designed to be a sophisticated system that leverages artificial intelligence to verify knowledge claims submitted to the K-Chain. By utilizing machine learning algorithms and natural language processing, Oracle AI can assess the validity of information and determine its relevance to the KBC network.

  • Relationship to KBC Foundations: Oracle AI is an integral component of the KBC platform, working in tandem with K-Chain, LightWeb, and Proof-of-Knowledge to create a robust ecosystem for storing and sharing verifiable knowledge. While K-Chain ensures the immutability of knowledge claims, LightWeb facilitates efficient information exchange, and Proof-of-Knowledge rewards contributors for their expertise.
