Education & Research on KBC

In the digital age, access to quality education and reliable research is more important than ever. Knowledge is power, and ensuring that information is accurate and accessible is crucial for personal growth, societal progress, and economic development. This is where Knowledge-Based Currency (KBC) comes in, offering a revolutionary platform that transforms verifiable knowledge into a secure digital asset.

Overview

  • K-Chain: K-Chain is the distributed ledger that stores immutable knowledge claims on the KBC platform. It serves as the foundation for recording and verifying information, ensuring that all knowledge transactions are secure and transparent.

  • LightWeb: LightWeb is a decentralized network that facilitates the efficient sharing of information on the KBC platform. It enables users to access a wide range of knowledge resources, fostering collaboration and innovation in education and research.

  • Oracle AI: Oracle AI is the intelligent engine that validates and retrieves knowledge on demand. It ensures that information is accurate and up-to-date, providing users with reliable answers to their queries.

  • Proof-of-Knowledge: KBC replaces traditional proofs of work or stake with Proof-of-Knowledge, rewarding contributors for their expertise. This incentivizes individuals to share their knowledge and expertise, creating a trustworthy and inclusive knowledge economy.

Conclusion
