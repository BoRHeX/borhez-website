# Decentralized Identity & Self-Sovereign Data: Empowering Individuals in the Knowledge Economy

In today's digital age, the issue of identity and data ownership has become increasingly critical. Individuals are constantly generating valuable data through their online interactions, yet they often lack control over how this data is used and shared. Decentralized identity and self-sovereign data solutions offer a way to empower individuals by giving them ownership and control over their personal information.

## Overview

Decentralized identity refers to the concept of individuals owning and managing their digital identities without relying on centralized authorities. Self-sovereign data builds upon this idea by putting individuals in control of their personal data and deciding who can access it.

- **K-Chain**: The distributed ledger at the core of the Knowledge-Based Currency (KBC) platform, K-Chain stores immutable knowledge claims securely. By leveraging K-Chain, individuals can create and manage their verifiable identities and data in a decentralized manner.
- **LightWeb**: A decentralized network for efficient information sharing, LightWeb enables individuals to exchange knowledge and data with others securely and efficiently.
- **Oracle AI**: The intelligent engine that validates and retrieves knowledge on demand, Oracle AI plays a crucial role in verifying the authenticity of identities and data in the KBC ecosystem.
- **Proof-of-Knowledge**: Instead of traditional proofs of work or stake, KBC uses Proof-of-Knowledge to reward contributors for their expertise. This incentivizes individuals to share their knowledge and data while ensuring the integrity of the network.
