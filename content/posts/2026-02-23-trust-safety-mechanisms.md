# Trust & Safety Mechanisms in the Knowledge-Based Currency (KBC) Movement

In a world where misinformation and fake news run rampant, establishing trust and ensuring safety are paramount. This is especially true in the realm of digital assets and decentralized networks like KBC, where the veracity of information is crucial. Trust & Safety Mechanisms play a vital role in the KBC ecosystem by safeguarding the integrity of knowledge claims and promoting a secure environment for all participants.

## Overview

- **K-Chain:** The distributed ledger where immutable knowledge claims are stored, ensuring transparency and accountability.
- **LightWeb:** A decentralized network for efficient sharing of information, facilitating the dissemination of knowledge across the KBC ecosystem.
- **Oracle AI:** An intelligent engine that validates and retrieves knowledge on demand, enhancing the accuracy and reliability of information.
- **Proof-of-Knowledge:** The mechanism that rewards contributors for their expertise, incentivizing the creation of trustworthy knowledge and promoting a fair knowledge economy.
