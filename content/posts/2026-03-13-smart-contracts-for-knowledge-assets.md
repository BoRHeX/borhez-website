## Overview

Smart contracts are self-executing contracts with the terms of the agreement directly written into code. These contracts automatically enforce and execute the terms of the agreement when predefined conditions are met, without the need for intermediaries. In the context of knowledge assets, smart contracts enable the secure and transparent exchange of verified knowledge between parties.

- **K-Chain**: The distributed ledger that stores immutable knowledge claims, ensuring the integrity and authenticity of knowledge assets.
- **LightWeb**: A decentralized network that facilitates efficient sharing of information, enabling seamless transactions of knowledge assets.
- **Oracle AI**: The intelligent engine that validates and retrieves knowledge on demand, ensuring the accuracy and reliability of knowledge assets.
- **Proof-of-Knowledge**: The mechanism that rewards contributors for their expertise and incentivizes the creation and sharing of valuable knowledge assets within the KBC ecosystem.
