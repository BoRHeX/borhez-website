# Trust & Safety Mechanisms in the Knowledge-Based Currency (KBC) Movement

Trust and safety mechanisms are crucial components of any digital platform, especially one like KBC that relies on verifiable knowledge as its core asset. In a world where misinformation and fake news abound, ensuring trust and safety in the exchange of knowledge is more important than ever. 

## Overview

In the KBC ecosystem, trust and safety mechanisms are built into every layer of the platform to ensure the integrity and reliability of the knowledge shared. Here's how each key component contributes to this:

- **K-Chain**: The distributed ledger where immutable knowledge claims are stored ensures that once information is recorded, it cannot be tampered with. This transparency and traceability build trust among users who can verify the authenticity of the knowledge shared.

- **LightWeb**: The decentralized network for sharing information efficiently ensures that knowledge is disseminated in a secure and reliable manner. By eliminating central points of control, LightWeb enhances the safety of the platform by reducing the risk of censorship or manipulation.

- **Oracle AI**: The intelligent engine that validates and retrieves knowledge on demand plays a crucial role in ensuring the accuracy and credibility of the information shared on KBC. By leveraging AI technology, KBC can quickly verify knowledge claims and provide users with reliable information.

- **Proof-of-Knowledge**: Instead of traditional proofs of work or stake, KBC rewards contributors based on their expertise and the quality of the knowledge they provide. This incentivizes users to share accurate and valuable information, fostering a trustworthy and inclusive knowledge economy.
