## Overview

Token launch strategies encompass the planning and execution of a cryptocurrency project's initial coin offering (ICO) or token sale. The goal is to attract investors, build a strong community, and ensure the long-term success of the project. In the case of KBC, token launch strategies are not just about fundraising but also about incentivizing knowledge sharing and rewarding expertise. Here's how the key components of KBC relate to token launch strategies:

- **K-Chain**: The distributed ledger that stores immutable knowledge claims plays a crucial role in token launch strategies by ensuring transparency and security in all transactions.
- **LightWeb**: The decentralized network for sharing information efficiently enables KBC to reach a wider audience during the token launch phase, fostering community engagement and knowledge dissemination.
- **Oracle AI**: The intelligent engine that validates and retrieves knowledge on demand ensures that the information shared during the token launch is accurate and trustworthy, building credibility and trust among investors.
- **Proof-of-Knowledge**: Instead of traditional proofs of work or stake, KBC rewards contributors for their expertise through Proof-of-Knowledge, aligning incentives with the platform's core mission of building a trustworthy and inclusive knowledge economy.
