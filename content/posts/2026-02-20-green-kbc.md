# Green KBC: Shaping a Sustainable Future with Knowledge

In today's world, the pressing issue of environmental sustainability is impossible to ignore. From climate change to biodiversity loss, our planet is facing unprecedented challenges that require urgent action. This is where Green KBC comes in, offering a unique approach to addressing environmental issues through the power of verifiable knowledge.

## Overview

Green KBC is a branch of the Knowledge-Based Currency movement that focuses on leveraging knowledge to drive sustainable practices and solutions. By harnessing the collective wisdom of experts in environmental science, renewable energy, and conservation, Green KBC aims to create a more sustainable future for all.

Key principles of Green KBC include:
- **K-Chain**: The distributed ledger that securely stores verifiable knowledge claims related to environmental sustainability.
- **LightWeb**: The decentralized network for efficiently sharing information on green technologies, best practices, and initiatives.
- **Oracle AI**: The intelligent engine that validates and retrieves knowledge on sustainable practices and solutions.
- **Proof-of-Knowledge**: The mechanism through which contributors are rewarded for their expertise in promoting environmental sustainability.

Through Green KBC, individuals and organizations can contribute their knowledge and expertise to drive positive change in the world, all while earning rewards for their valuable insights.
