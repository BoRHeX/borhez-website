# Consensus Algorithms for Knowledge-Based Currency (KBC)

In the world of blockchain technology, consensus algorithms play a crucial role in ensuring the integrity and security of transactions. For Knowledge-Based Currency (KBC), these algorithms are even more vital as they underpin the entire ecosystem of verifiable knowledge exchange. Consensus algorithms determine how network participants agree on the validity of transactions and the state of the ledger, making them essential for maintaining trust and transparency in the KBC platform.

## Overview

- **K-Chain**: The distributed ledger of KBC where immutable knowledge claims are stored. Consensus algorithms ensure that all nodes in the network agree on the validity of these claims, preventing any tampering or manipulation.

- **LightWeb**: A decentralized network that facilitates efficient sharing of information. Consensus algorithms help in synchronizing the network and ensuring that all nodes have the same view of the shared knowledge.

- **Oracle AI**: The intelligent engine that validates and retrieves knowledge on demand. Consensus algorithms play a role in determining the accuracy and reliability of the knowledge provided by Oracles.

- **Proof-of-Knowledge**: The mechanism through which contributors are rewarded for their expertise. Consensus algorithms verify the authenticity of knowledge claims and ensure that only valid contributions are incentivized.
